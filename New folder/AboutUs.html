About NIT Warangal

The National Institute of Technology, Warangal (NITW), established in 1959, stands as the first of the Regional Engineering Colleges in India. In 2002, it was accorded the status of a National Institute of Technology and in 2007, declared an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. Since its inception, NIT Warangal has been at the forefront of technical education, research, and innovation, shaping generations of engineers, scientists, and leaders.


Location and Campus

NIT Warangal is located in Warangal, Telangana, approximately 150 km from Hyderabad, well-connected by road and rail. The Institute’s 256-acre residential campus combines state-of-the-art academic infrastructure with lush greenery, providing an inspiring environment for learning and growth. The campus houses academic departments, advanced laboratories, research centers, libraries, hostels, sports complexes, and modern amenities that support both academic and personal development.


Academic Excellence

NIT Warangal offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines of engineering, sciences, and management. The Institute is known for its rigorous academic curriculum, experienced faculty, and emphasis on research and innovation.


Research and Innovation

Warangal has a vibrant research culture supported by Centers of Excellence, government-funded projects, and collaborations with industry and academia worldwide. The Institute promotes entrepreneurship through its Innovation and Incubation Center, enabling students and researchers to transform ideas into impactful solutions.


Student Life and Diversity

Home to over 7,000 students from all states of India and abroad, NIT Warangal is a microcosm of cultural and intellectual diversity. The campus is alive with technical festivals, cultural events, entrepreneurship summits, hackathons, and sports tournaments, encouraging holistic growth beyond academics.


Faculty and Alumni

The faculty at NITW comprises distinguished scholars, researchers, and professionals committed to teaching and knowledge creation. Its alumni network, spread across the globe, includes leaders in technology, academia, entrepreneurship, public service, and corporate sectors, continuing to contribute significantly to society.


Vision & Mission

Guided by its motto “Progress Through Knowledge,” NIT Warangal aims to:

  • Impart world-class technical and scientific education
  • research and innovation addressing societal and industrial challenges
  • Nurture socially responsible, globally competent professionals and leaders
  • Contribute to sustainable national and international development
