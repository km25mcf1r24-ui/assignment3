National Institute of Technology, Warangal

Departments and Courses


The National Institute of Technology Warangal has several departments which offer varios undergraduate, graduate and research level courses for students to sharpen their skills and knowledge in various technical and theoretical fields of engineering and sciences. The departments not only aim to impart quality education and skills to the students but also train them to think critically, adapt to evolving challenges, and uphold values of integrity, empathy, and innovation. Through a blend of rigorous academics, hands-on projects, and community engagement, students are prepared to become not just professionals, but thoughtful leaders and lifelong learners.
