Welcome to National Institute of Technolody, Warangal!















The National Institute of Technology Warangal (NIT-Warangal or NIT-W) is a public technical and research university located in Warangal, Telangana, India. It is recognised as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. The foundation stone for this institute was laid by then Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru on 10 October 1959, the first in the chain of 31 NITs (formerly known as RECs) in the country. The institute was renamed as the National Institute of Technology, Warangal in 2002. NIT Warangal is ranked as one of the prestigious engineering institutions in India. The institute is committed to excellence in education, research, and community service.


The institute has produced several alumni who have contributed to the social and technological development of the society. The institute continues to strive in imparting high quality education and research.


The institute cordially invites you to join its mission.
